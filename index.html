SHaRe-RL

Structured, Human-in-the-Loop Reinforcement Learning for Contact-Rich Industrial Assembly Tasks

Abstract

We present SHaRe-RL, a reinforcement learning framework for high-precision, contact-rich industrial assembly. By integrating manipulation primitives, human-in-the-loop corrections, and adaptive compliance, SHaRe-RL enables safe and sample-efficient learning in the real world. Experiments with industrial connectors show high success within practical time budgets, outperforming human baselines.
